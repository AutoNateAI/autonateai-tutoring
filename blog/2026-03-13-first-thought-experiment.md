# Thought Experiment #1: The Topology of Success

Welcome to the first **AutoNateAI Thought Experiment**. Today, we're diving into the relationship between **Graph Theory** and your **Career Path**.

## The Problem

Imagine your career as a directed acyclic graph (DAG). Each node is a skill or a course. Each edge represents a prerequisite or a connection between concepts.

**Your Goal:** Find the longest path in this graph. What does it represent? Is it the "hardest" route, or the one with the most value?

### The Challenge

1.  **Clone the Starter Repo:** `https://github.com/AutoNateAI/challenge-dag-mastery` (Placeholder)
2.  **Analyze the UML/Mermaid Designs:** Inside the `/docs` folder, you'll find the architecture for a "Career Path Optimizer."
3.  **Submit Your Code:** Implement the `findLongestPath` algorithm and submit a Pull Request.

## Why This Matters

Companies aren't looking for people who can just code. They want engineers who understand the **topology** of their systems. If you can solve this, you're one step closer to mastering the "Software Engineer" node in your journey.
