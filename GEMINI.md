# AutoNateAI | Project Standing Orders

## 🎯 The Vision
AutoNateAI is a high-stakes "Edutainment" harbor designed to convert humans into deep thinkers. We transform **LeetCode/HackerRank** patterns into immersive, character-driven **Thought Experiments** (Quests) that bridge theoretical epiphanies and industrial reality.

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture
*   **Framework:** Docusaurus (TypeScript). Serves from `https://autonateai.com`.
*   **Theme:** **Dark Mode is mandatory**. High-contrast white text in the hero.
*   **Hero Visualization:** Custom animated SVG (Topological Web) showing the path to **MASTERY**.
*   **Funnels:** Strategy Sessions are linked to specific **Google Appointment Schedules** ($150/$200/$250 tiers).

## ⚔️ The Gold Standard: Mission Campaigns
All future quests must emulate **Operation: Silicon Deadlock**.
*   **Narrative:** 1200+ words, action-packed, culturally relevant (sports, finance, politics).
*   **Visuals:** Exactly **9 cinematic 16:9 images** (Disney/Pixar style).
*   **Character Consistency:** Maya, Leo, and Dr. Aris must look identical across all scenes using the `openai-gpt-image` skill with character anchors.
*   **Structure:** 5 sections starting with a `<CardSlam />` hook. **NO "ACT" NUMBERING** in headers.
*   **Code:** Scaled-down blocks for mobile UX (font-size: 0.65rem).

## 🛠️ Infrastructure & Skills
*   **`thought-experiment-architect`:** Use this to generate the 5-act epic content.
*   **`openai-gpt-image`:** Use for high-fidelity `gpt-image-1.5` generation with `fidelity="high"`.
*   **`og-image-architect`:** Use for generating wordless, captivating social previews.
*   **`gws-sheets`:** Integrated with the "Curriculum Intelligence" workbook for CRM and logging.
