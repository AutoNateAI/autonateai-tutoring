# Your CS Journey Mastery Map

Welcome to the **AutoNateAI CS Journey**. This is not a replacement for your college curriculum—it's the nervous system that connects it to the real world.

## Why Map Your Journey?

Most CS students feel like they are collecting disconnected facts. We help you visualize how your courses, projects, and research fit into the topological map of becoming a professional software engineer.

### The Topological Approach

Unlike a linear syllabus, our map is hierarchical and interconnected:
- **Foundations:** Discrete Math, Data Structures, Algorithms.
- **Systems:** Operating Systems, Networking, Databases.
- **Craft:** Software Design Patterns, Clean Code, CI/CD.
- **Frontier:** AI Engineering, Distributed Systems, High-Performance Computing.

## How to Use This Map

1. **Mark Your Progress:** Select the nodes (topics) you've covered in your classes.
2. **Identify Gaps:** See exactly what's missing between your current state and your career goals.
3. **Unlock Experiments:** Based on your marked topics, we generate personalized **Thought Experiments** to test your mastery.
